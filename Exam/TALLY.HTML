TALLY Course

Online Test

Eligibility:

Open to students who have completed 10th or 12th grade.
Basic knowledge of accounting or mathematics is helpful but not mandatory.
Generally 1 to 6 months, depending on the level of the course (basic, advanced, or specialized modules).

Syllabus for TALLY Course


Curriculum:

The course is divided into theoretical and practical components, covering:

Basic Tally:

Introduction to Tally software.
Setting up a company profile in Tally.
Recording day-to-day financial transactions.
Understanding ledgers, vouchers, and trial balances.

Advanced Tally:

GST (Goods and Services Tax) implementation and filing.
Inventory management.
Payroll management.
Cost centers and budgeting.

Specialized Modules:

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) and TCS (Tax Collected at Source).
MIS (Management Information Systems) reporting.
Multi-currency transactions.
Bank reconciliation and e-banking integration.

Benefits of the ADCA Course:

◘Tally proficiency is a valuable skill in the job market.
◘It is widely used by businesses of all sizes for accounting and compliance.
◘Helps in managing personal or business accounts effectively.


Examination Details:

The exam format typically includes theory questions and practical tasks.

Theoretical Section:
Multiple-choice questions (MCQs) or short-answer questions on accounting concepts, Tally features, and taxation.
Practical Section:
Tasks such as creating ledgers, entering transactions, generating reports, and managing GST.
Typically, candidates need to score 50%-60% or higher to pass the exam.
Exact criteria depend on the training institute.
