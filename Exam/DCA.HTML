DCA Course

Online Test

Eligibility:

Open to students who have completed their 10th or 12th grade.
No prior knowledge of computers is required.
Generally 6 months to 1 year, depending on the institution.

Syllabus for DCA Course


Curriculum:

The DCA course focuses on fundamental computer skills. Topics typically include:

Computer Fundamentals:

Introduction to computer hardware, software, and peripherals.

Operating Systems:

Basics of Windows, Linux, or macOS.

MS Office Suite:

MS Word:

Word processing and document formatting.

MS Excel:

Spreadsheet creation, formulas, and data handling.

MS PowerPoint:

Presentation creation and editing.

Internet Applications:

Email communication, browsing, and online collaboration tools.
Introduction to Programming: Basic understanding of programming concepts (optional).
Database Management: Basics of databases using MS Access or similar tools.
Tally: Basic accounting software usage (optional in some programs).

Benefits of the ADCA Course:

◘Provides a strong foundation for beginners.
◘Equips learners with essential computer skills required in almost every profession.
◘Can be completed in a short time, making it ideal for those seeking immediate skill enhancement.
◘ Prepares students for entry-level IT jobs or further studies in computer science.

Examination Details:

Conducting Authority:


NIELIT Mode of Examination: Online or offline, as decided by NIELIT
Grading System:
Assignments and exams are graded, and candidates must achieve passing marks in both practical and theory components.
