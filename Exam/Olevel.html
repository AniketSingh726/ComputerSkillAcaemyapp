O-LEVEL Course

Online Test

Eligibility:

10+2 or ITI certificate (one year) after class 10:
Candidates must have completed high school education or its equivalent.
Alternatively, candidates who have completed the NIELIT 'CCC' course can also apply.
Course Duration:
The course typically takes 1 year to complete, divided into 2 semesters of 6 months each.

Syllabus for O-Level Course


Theory Papers:

M1-R5: IT Tools and Network Basics

Basics of computer hardware and software
Operating systems and networking
Microsoft Office tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
M2-R5: Web Designing & Publishing

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
Basics of web hosting
Building responsive websites
M3-R5: Programming and Problem Solving Through Python

Introduction to Python programming
Problem-solving techniques
Handling files and data
M4-R5: Internet of Things (IoT) and its Applications

Basics of IoT devices
IoT communication protocols
IoT-based application development

Practical and Project Work:

Practical Work:


Hands-on experience in topics covered during the course, such as MS Office, Python programming, and web designing.

Project Work:


A mandatory project based on one of the subjects studied. Students must submit a report to complete the course.

Examination Details:

Conducting Authority:


NIELIT Mode of Examination: Online or offline, as decided by NIELIT
Grading System:
Assignments and exams are graded, and candidates must achieve passing marks in both practical and theory components.
