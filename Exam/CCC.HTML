CCC Course

Online Test

Eligibility:

No specific educational qualification is required.
Open to anyone interested in learning basic computer skills.

Typically 80 hours of training:
Theory: 25 hours
Practical: 50 hours
Tutorials: 5 hours
This may span around 2 to 3 months, depending on the institution.

Syllabus for CCC Course


Curriculum:

The CCC syllabus focuses on basic computer literacy and includes:

Introduction to Computers:

Understanding hardware, software, and operating systems.

Operating System Usage:

Basics of using Windows or Linux environments.

MS Office Tools:

MS Word:

Document creation and editing.

MS Excel:

Spreadsheets and basic data handling.

MS PowerPoint:

Creating and managing presentations.

Internet Applications:

Browsing, using search engines, and email management.
Basics of e-governance and accessing online services.

Digital Payments:

Using mobile banking, UPI, and e-wallets.

Cybersecurity Awareness:

Basic knowledge of online safety and security.

Benefits of the ADCA Course:

◘Provides basic computer literacy.
◘Helps in obtaining government jobs where CCC certification is often mandatory.
◘Equips individuals with skills for basic computer operations and digital transactions.

Examination Details:

The CCC certification is conducted by NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology) in India.
Exams are held every month in online mode.
The exam consists of 100 multiple-choice questions, and candidates need at least 50% marks to pass.
