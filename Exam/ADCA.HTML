ADCA Course

Online Test

Eligibility:

Open to students who have completed 10th or 12th grade.
Basic knowledge of computers is helpful but not mandatory.
Typically 12 months, but it may vary depending on the institution offering the course.

Syllabus for ADCA Course


Curriculum:

The course usually includes both theoretical and practical components, covering the following topics:

Fundamentals of Computers:

Understanding computer hardware, software, and operating systems.

Office Tools:

Training in MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access).

Programming Languages:

Introduction to languages like C, C++, Java, Python, etc.

Web Designing:

Basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Database Management:

Overview of databases, SQL, and tools like MS Access or MySQL.

Accounting Software:

Training in tools like Tally.

Graphic Design:

Basics of Photoshop, CorelDRAW, etc. (optional in some versions of the course).

Networking Concepts:

Basics of computer networking and internet usage.

Cybersecurity Awareness:

Introduction to online safety and basic cybersecurity.

Benefits of the ADCA Course:

◘Provides practical knowledge of computer operations and applications.
◘Opens doors to entry-level IT jobs.
◘Prepares students for more specialized IT courses.
◘Builds essential skills for modern office environments.

Examination Details:

Conducting Authority:


NIELIT Mode of Examination: Online or offline, as decided by NIELIT
Grading System:
Assignments and exams are graded, and candidates must achieve passing marks in both practical and theory components.
